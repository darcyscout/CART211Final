Wild Stories

The freshwater river barely flowed around the scaly beast. Its monstrous tail ever so slowly paddled underwater like a suspended ribbon of a graceful dancer. It had been hungry for months now.

A couple unfortunate lizards swam through last week, but it wasn’t enough. The memory of those snacks was soon overwritten by intense hunger.

Every tissue in her body ached beautify. It was the most comforting feeling. It can only be described as perfect bliss.

Ever since she hatched, the feeling has been there.

The never ending hunger has always kept her afloat, nose and eyes poking from the membrane of the water’s surface. She’d mastered the perfect floating drift, even at that age. What she is now is beyond mastery, it’s perfection.

No one ever had to train her. If she could see out of those watchful eyes, she knew exactly what to do.

The wait is glorious, but the kill is divine.

It comes at a cost. Each time she senses a potential victim, the blissful drift ceases. As it becomes more and more clear she has an opportune kill a tension fills her. Not anxiety but pure tension. Her muscles don’t betray her through, and as the pressure builds it becomes unbearable until…

She unleashes at the climax with a ferocious bite that could pin down a god. And when it lands, the rush of pleasure is so great that the memory cannot be held.

She eats, a moment passes… I’m hungry’
